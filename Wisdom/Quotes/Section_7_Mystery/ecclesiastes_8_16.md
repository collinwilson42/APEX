# Quote

"When I applied my mind to know wisdom, I saw all that God has done. No one can comprehend what goes on under the sun."

## Context

The Preacher observes that despite applying his mind to wisdom, he cannot comprehend everything. Some things remain mysterious. Complete understanding is impossible.
