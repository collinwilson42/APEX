# Quote

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding."

## Context

This verse counsels against over-reliance on your own analysis and judgment. While intelligence and planning are valuable, Solomon warns against trusting solely in your own understanding without considering broader perspectives or the possibility you're wrong.
