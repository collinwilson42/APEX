# Quote

"Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their labor: If either of them falls down, one can help the other up."

## Context

The Preacher (traditionally Solomon) reflects on the value of partnership and community. Working together produces better returns than working alone. When one person struggles or falls, having support makes the critical difference.
