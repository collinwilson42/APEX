# Quote

"A house divided against itself cannot stand."

## Context

Solomon observes that internal conflict and division destroys from within. A house, family, business, or mind that is divided against itself cannot stand under pressure. Unity and coherence are necessary for strength.

## Notes

In trading: If your strategy conflicts with your risk management, or your emotions conflict with your plan, you'll fail. Internal coherence between strategy, execution, and psychology is essential.
